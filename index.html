Guidelines for Using the Complaint System
  • Step 1: Register yourself by visiting the User Registration page.
  • Step 2: After registration, log in using the User Login page.
  • Step 3: Set your personal information in your profile.
  • Step 4: In the complaint section, choose the type and sub-type of your complaint.
  • Step 5: Write your complaint and submit it. Wait for a response.
  • Step 6: You can check the status of your complaint on your dashboard.
  • Step 7: To log out, click the Logout button located in the top-right corner of the page.
